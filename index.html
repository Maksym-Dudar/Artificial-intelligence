Self-driving car

A self-driving car is a vehicle that uses a combination of sensors, cameras, radar, and artificial intelligence to travel between destinations without a human operator. To qualify as fully autonomous, a vehicle must be capable of driving without human intervention to a predetermined destination on roads that were not designed for its use.
